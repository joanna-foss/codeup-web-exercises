The Solar Gun is a weapon awarded for finding the five Beauty Beast Corps statues - you are awarded them provided you defeat the 4 Beast forms: Laughing Octopus, Raging Raven, Crying Wolf and Screaming Mantis with stun draining weapons. Additionally, you cannot kill any of the BB Corps' Frog Soldiers when making your escape with Meryl Silverburgh's team at the hotel in Act 1 (the Frog statue appears at the start of the next area). Note that the criteria for the Solar Gun is different from the Big Boss emblem criteria; the Big Boss emblem allows you to use lethal weapons on the Beast form, but non-lethal weapons on the Beauties.
